What's our goal?

The goal of the Mindful Reader App is to aid readers in their retention and recall of their favourite books by developing a sustainable reading and journaling routine.

Definition

User Insight Statement

During our user interviews, we discovered that users are struggling to build a healthy habit of reading and journalling consistently causing them to have difficulty in retaining the book that they've read. Therefore, we believe that users need to have a habit tracking platform and that we might be able to help if we create an application that reminds users to read and prompt users to journal. We might do this by reminding users to read and journal via notifications, providing templates for journal entries, and utilising icons to create a visual method of tracking. Doing this will allow our product to help users to build a sustainable habit of reading and journalling which increases retention of books that they've read.

Problem Statement

It is difficult for users to have consistent habit for reading and to remember what they've previously read. Our research showed that journaling is effective in memory retention and recall, however it can be hard to find time to journal or know how to write a journal entry. Journaling about what you've read will help users to remember, giving them confidence and encouraging them to read more often.
